Hello! Global Health Summit 2015

WORLD HEALTH
SUMMIT 2023

The health sector is constantly evolving, and new challenges continue to emerge. As such, it is important to develop a comprehensive health development plan that can guide efforts to improve public health outcomes. Such a plan should incorporate current best practices, leverage innovative technologies and approaches, and prioritize evidence-based interventions. One key element of a health development plan for the future is the need to address health inequalities. In many countries, certain populations, such as low-income and marginalized groups, experience poorer health outcomes compared to others.

Vanue : Mirpur national stadium, Dhaka Bangladesh.
